About

I'm Ruhani — a brand and marketing professional finishing my MSc in Management at the University of Birmingham. I live at the intersection of commercial thinking and creative instinct, which is where I think brands actually get built.

I've worked across digital marketing, content strategy, business operations, and financial analysis — meaning I understand how a business runs, not just how it looks.
